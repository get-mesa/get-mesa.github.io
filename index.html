How is Mesa different?

MESA is not social media, instant messaging, nor a CRM

Mesa is nothing like traditional social media; there is no public profile to "groom", no "feed" to keep you glued and distracted, no ads to annoy you, no algorithm judging your content's value, no business model based on selling your clicks and attention.

Messaging platforms work great for chatting often with those we remember easily, but fail to organize our conversations around our needs. Mesa helps you learn what matters to you with the right people, and avoid the chaos and forgetfulness of instant messages.

CRMs are good for “pushing” conversations into business deals, but they feel fake and annoying when used for other relationships. Like CRMs, Mesa also reminds you of who you know, but triggers simple, natural and respectul exchanges with your friends.
