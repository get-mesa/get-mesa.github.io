WHAT YOU NEED FROM WHO YOU TRUST

Entrepreneurs are always looking for new information, so they can avoid mistakes and jump on the best opportunities. Mesa helps you get the most useful and private recommendations and advice from your smartest connections.

THE "BIG NETWORK" PROBLEM

MESA MAKES LIFE EASIER FOR THE HIGHLY CONNECTED ENTREPRENEUR
"WHO DO I KNOW?"

Digital entrepreneurs know tons of people! It’s hard to remember who to ask for help sometimes, and scrolling through a list of contacts is a pain.

"WHAT SHOULD I KNOW?"

The news and social media may be entertaining, but are slow and distracting ways to find the best information for our situation.

"WHEN SHOULD I ASK?"

Busy people want to help you, but sometimes they forget you asked, forget what or who they know, or remember it too late!

ENTREPRENEURS ARE BUSY PEOPLE

MESA RESPECTS YOUR TIME, FOCUS, AND CONVENIENCE
Quick & Simple

Easy to send questions, answers or updates to your most important connections.

STORE & REMEMBER

Save, organize and rediscover what your allies told you.

NOT DISTRACTING

We don't steal your attention. Get in, do what you need, and get out.

MULTI-LEVEL PRIVACY

Relationships are complex. Navigate them with new options of privacy and shareability.

SELECTIVE

No "public profile", no requests from strangers, no long lists of disjointed messages.

RELEVANT

No endless feeds of "news" and ads. Focus on what matters for your goals and your allies’ needs.
